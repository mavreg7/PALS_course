ETT Sizing & Depth

Formulas to Memorize

Internal diameter (mm)

  • Cuffed: (age / 4) + 3.5
  • Uncuffed: (age / 4) + 4
  • Newborn: 3.0-3.5 mm
  • Verify with Broselow tape if available

Insertion depth at lip (cm)

  • (age / 2) + 12 · or 3 × ETT size
  • Newborn: 6 + weight (kg)
  • Confirm equal breath sounds + CXR

Confirming Placement

Five-Point Check

  1. Visualize cords during intubation.
  2. Continuous waveform ETCO₂ — gold standard.
  3. Symmetric bilateral breath sounds, no epigastric sounds.
  4. Symmetric chest rise; condensation in tube.
  5. Chest x-ray after stabilization; tip 2 cm above carina.

DOPES for sudden deterioration: Displacement · Obstruction · Pneumothorax · Equipment · Stacked breaths.

Clinical Pearl

"Plan the airway, then plan the rescue."

Always know your Plan B before you push the paralytic. A working LMA, a difficult-airway kit, and a clear surgical airway exit save lives when the first attempt fails.
