2025 AHA / ILCOR Highlights

Seven Changes Worth Memorizing

1
Simultaneous breathing + pulse check ≤10 s (was sequential).
2
Physiology-directed CPR: use ETCO2 (≥15-20 mmHg) and DBP (≥25 infants, ≥30 children) when available.
3
Expanded reversible causes include intracranial hypertension and heart block.
4
IV sotalol endorsed for refractory SVT after adenosine + 1 antiarrhythmic.
5
Unified chain of survival integrating IHCA and OHCA flows.
6
Written exam is open-resource — provider manual & 2025 guidelines permitted.
7
ECPR reasonable in IHCA with reversible cause + ECMO expertise.

Clinical Pearl

"Compress hard, monitor what you can."

When invasive monitoring is available, let the patient's physiology guide CPR quality — DBP and ETCO2 are real-time feedback that outperform a stopwatch alone.
